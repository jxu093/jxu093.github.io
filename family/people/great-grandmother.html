Paternal Great-Grandmother

Generation I · Shandong → Dalian → Jilin
Shandong, China

Life Story

She migrated from Shandong to Dalian with her husband, who found work as a port laborer. After his early death left the family without income, she relocated the family to Jilin to live with relatives. There they rented a house and land cheaply to grow their own food for survival.

During the Japanese occupation of the 1940s, she raised Xu Shidong under extreme hardship in Jilin. Kind Japanese neighbors helped the family avoid starvation.
