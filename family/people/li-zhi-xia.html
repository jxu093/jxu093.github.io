The Chinese characters 李志霞 are the most likely reading of "Li Zhi Xia" — 志 (aspiration) and 霞 (rosy clouds) are common in women's names of this era — but have not been confirmed.

Life Story

Li Zhi Xia grew up in Dalian with her father, step-mother (who joined the family long ago and was considered their true mother), and three brothers. Her biological mother's details are not known.

She met Xu Qiang (James) at an English language school in Dalian, where both were preparing to emigrate. They married and lived in a company-provided apartment called Yi Mao Gou before emigrating to Canada together in June 2000.

Family in Dalian

Her father has since passed away. Her step-mother, who had been in the family for a long time and was considered the children's true mother, passed away in 2026. Her three brothers are believed to still be based in Dalian.
