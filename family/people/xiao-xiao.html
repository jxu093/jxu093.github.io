Life & Business

Xiao Xiao lives in Beijing with her husband Ye Xiao, whose family is also from Beijing. Together they run their own accessories business: she designs the products, has them produced at a factory, and sells them — promoting everything through Douyin (China's TikTok), where she has built a large following.

The business is very successful. They have a newly built four-floor home in Beijing with an elevator inside. They work long hours.
