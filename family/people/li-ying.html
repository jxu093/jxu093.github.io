Life in Lanzhou

Li Ying built a successful career as a high-level sales leader at Mary Kay in Lanzhou — a senior role that came with prestige and annual company trips abroad. Destinations included Hawaii and Europe. She had never been to Canada before meeting James.

Meeting James & Emigration

She met Xu Qiang (James) through a mutual friend in 2018 and they married approximately 2019–2020. During the COVID pandemic, James came to live with her in China. After he returned to Canada, she obtained Canadian permanent residency through marriage (~2023–2024) and has been living with him in Canada since.

Since relocating, she has scaled back her direct Mary Kay role but continues to earn income by participating in team meetings remotely.
