Education

Raised in Yingkou, Xu Qiang (James) attended university in Shanghai, graduating with a degree in refrigeration and cooling. The government initially assigned him to teach at a post-high school technical college in Dalian — a role he did not enjoy.

The Turning Point: Australia

Through his friend Mr. Feng, he secured a position at an international seafood company in Dalian's new commerce district. The company sent him to Australia for a month.

In Australia, he met a local man named Steve — only one year older — who already owned a house, a car, and played golf. Comparing that to the lack of opportunity in China at the time, he decided to emigrate.

Emigration to Canada

He met Li Zhi Xia at an English language school in Dalian, where both were preparing to emigrate. They lived in a company-provided apartment called Yi Mao Gou. He chose Canada over Australia (felt too small) and New Zealand, and could not access the US due to strict skill requirements.

The move was planned for 1999 but was delayed by a car accident involving Jerry. The family left for Canada in June 2000, just as his visa was about to expire.

Later Life

He met Li Ying through a mutual friend in 2018 and they married approximately 2019–2020. During the COVID pandemic, he went to live with her in China. He later returned to Canada, and Li Ying obtained Canadian permanent residency through marriage (~2023–2024) and joined him there.
