His Name

His Chinese name, 许一来 (Xǔ Yī Lái), was chosen with intention: 一 means "first" — he is the firstborn — and 来 means "to come," marking his arrival. Together: the first to come. The name also happens to echo the sound of "Eli," his English nickname.

The middle name AJ honours both of his grandfathers: Angelo Parago (Emmylou's father) and James — Xu Qiang (Jerry's father). It is a deliberate bridge between the two family lines meeting in him.

His Heritage

Elijia will carry four generations of two very different journeys in a single person:

On his father's side: a family line that began in Shandong, moved through Dalian and Jilin under Japanese occupation, served in the Communist revolution, survived frozen trenches, and eventually emigrated to Canada in June 2000.

On his mother's side: a Filipino-Canadian family with roots in Manila and Cebu, who built a life in Mississauga.
