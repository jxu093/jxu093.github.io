Her birthday, the 15th day of the 7th lunar month, is also 中元节 — the Ghost Festival, one of the most significant dates in the Chinese lunar calendar.

Life Story

Wang Guilan lived and worked in Yingkou at a clothing factory. She was known as a strictly upright citizen — during an era when factory theft was common, she actively reprimanded her coworkers for stealing rather than turning a blind eye.

She was introduced to Xu Shidong by one of her uncles, a factory manager, who thought her future husband was "very handsome." They married and had three children together: two daughters and Xu Qiang (James).

She passed away in 2003, before many of her grandchildren could know her well.
