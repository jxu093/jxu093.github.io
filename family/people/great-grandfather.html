Life Story

The earliest known branch of the family. He migrated from Shandong province to Dalian seeking work, finding employment as a manual laborer at the Dalian port docks — loading and unloading ships.

He died while his son (Xu Shidong) was still a child. As the sole breadwinner, his early death meant the family could no longer afford to survive in the city. They were forced to relocate to Jilin to live with relatives, where they rented land cheaply to grow food for survival. His death set the entire chain of the family's journey in motion.
