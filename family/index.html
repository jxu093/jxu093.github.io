Japanese Occupation — Family Moves to Jilin

After great-grandfather's death, the family relocates to Jilin to live with relatives, renting land to grow food for survival. Despite the occupation, kind Japanese neighbors help the family avoid starvation.

~1936

Serves as a young officer fighting the KMT and mountain raiders. During one engagement, he spends three days lying in frozen trenches — his leg is permanently injured from the cold.

~1950s
